The Periodic Table of Elements

The Periodic Table of Elements is a large chart that labels (, well) the elements. Our universe is partly made out of elements, or matter, the rest is just random bits of inter-stellar space that are invisible for the most part. Every element has a name, symbol, atomic number, and atomic weight, this is mostly shown on the periodic table
